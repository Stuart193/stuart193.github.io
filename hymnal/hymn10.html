10

All People That on Earth Do Dwell

Tunes
VERSE 1-All people that on earth do dwell,
sing to the Lord with cheerful voice.
Him serve with joy, His praise forth-tell.
Come we before Him, and rejoice!

VERSE 2-Know that the Lord is God indeed;
without our aid He did us make.
We are His folk, He doth us feed,
and for His sheep He doth us take.

VERSE 3-O enter then His gates with praise;
approach with joy His courts unto.
Praise, laud, and bless His name always,
for it is seemly so to do.

VERSE 4-For why? The Lord our God is good:
His mercy is forever sure,
His truth at all times firmly stood,
and shall from age to age endure.

VERSE 5-To Father, Son, and Holy Ghost,
the God Whom Heav'n and earth adore,
from us and from the angel host
be praise and glory evermore.
